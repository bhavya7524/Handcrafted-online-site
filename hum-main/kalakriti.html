Why Handmade?

At kalakriti,we obsess over customers and their desire for a trusted destination for handcrafted goods, which is what prompted us to start Handmade.Together, we are growing craft communities and successful businesses.

Why Indian Crafts?

The crafts of India are diverse, rich in history, culture and religion. The craft of each state in India reflect the influence of different empires. Throughout centuries, crafts have been embedded as a culture and tradition within rural communities.

Some Facts about Handmade Crafts

The most common handicrafts in India date back to more than 3000 years ago and have evolved into many different forms over the years such as carpets and rugs (handmade), embroidered fabrics (machine-made), furniture (handmade) etc.
